Goku

Goku is a saiyan which is an alien or basically a being not from Earth that is naturally inclined to be combative and physically stronger than humans. This life-form age at a decreased rate compared to humans and enjoy the activity of hand-to-hand combat as well as energy manipulation.

Vegeta

Vegeta is a saiyan which is an alien or basically a being not from Earth that is naturally inclined to be combative and physically stronger than humans. Saiyans age at an decreased rate compared to humans and enjoy the activity of hand-to-hand combat as well as energy manipulation. Vegeta is a genius when it involves combat and is also consider an elite type of Saiyan which provides him with unique characteristics that the manga/anime have yet to expound upon.

Naruto

Naruto, is an shinobi which is basically a assassin/spy/warrior for his village. What's exceptional about this fiction character is that he is a vessel of an extreme violet entity that once terrorized his village. Although, he was initially shunned by the locals of his village, Naruto's overal stubborn but positive attitude and nurturing surrogate family resulted in his eventual succession as the seventh leader of his village!

Luffy

This human was altered by strange but mystical fruit that creates strong warriors but at a price. Luffy is on an continual quest to obtain the most prize treasure in his world called One Piece. That's right, Luffy is a young pirate who embarks on a journey to obtain the title of Pirate King. So far in his journey he has gained many allies as well as enemies!
